🚀 Features

* Multiple accounts 👥
* Works offline 🔌
* Manage cards, lists and boards 📝
* Manage tags 🏷
* Supports attachments 📎, activities ⚡ & comments 💬
* Dark mode 🌙
* Translated in many languages 🌎


🔗 Requirements

  * Nextcloud (https://nextcloud.com/)
  * Nextcloud Deck app (https://apps.nextcloud.com/apps/deck)
  * Nextcloud Android app (https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nextcloud.client)
